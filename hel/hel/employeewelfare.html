Human Capital is the company’s greatest asset and the backbone of any organization. Research shows, there is an economic link between employee satisfaction and company financial performance. A happy workplace culture does translate into better bottom-line of the Organization.

Happy Employees = Happy Customers = Happy Shareholders.

At HEL, we believe in nurturing our employees, developing them for tomorrow’s challenges, by creating a healthy work atmosphere and providing them with the tools and necessary support to do their jobs effectively.

The HR Function of Haldia Energy Limited ensures that, ensures all the verticals are working in alignment with the Organizational Goals on one hand and the employee development on the other hand.The overall Well-being of an employee is of utmost to maximise productivity in our Plants and at Corporate. HEL promotes an environment that helps to develop a sense of connection, belongingness and a well-developed support system to bind the employees together towards achievement of Organizational goals.

Intellectual Wellness

Timely and need based Learning & Development opportunities, On the Job Assignments, Projects ensures the intellectual wellness for the employees. A well thought of L & D Philosophy – 1:1:1 is followed at HEL, such that every employee attends a minimum number of Training based upon his need, his Manager’s Input and the Organizational Need.. A well-designed Rewards and Recognition Scheme has been put in place to enhance the culture of appreciation whenever there is an opportunity of appreciation. Spot Awards (Ulka) and Employee of the Month Awards (Dhruv) are encouraging employees to raise the bar and actively participate in making the workplace better.

To ensure trust & transparency, leadership ensures that there are various mode/channels of Communication provided to employees namely Plant Communication Meetings, Town Hall & Departmental Meetings to ensure that all the requisite information goes down to the last level. Our strength lies in various two-way Communication processes, wherein, from Top Management to the Department level meetings ensures the seamless flow of Communication, which helps us to understand the minds and needs of our employees.

Physical Wellness

Yoga Sessions, sports functions (both indoor and outdoor) are scheduled at specific intervals to ensure physical wellness. Medical and Safety Awareness Sessions are done at periodic intervals to educate employees on healthy habits. Good health, medical policies as a support with family coverage Ensures environmental wellness; Executive Health check Up ; Health Insurance and Medical Assistance policies, Medical Camps are some initiatives towards environmental wellness of our employees.

Some of the activities that we organize for our employees are – Walkathon, Quiz Competitions, Sports activities, festival celebrations, Annual Day Celebrations, milestone achievements and the like.

Environmental Wellness

Creation of a High-Performance Work environment is a Strategic HR intent and Employee wellbeing and Employee engagement is of paramount Importance for achieving the objective. Employee Safety is of paramount importance to HEL and Implementation of OHSAS is a very decisive step in this direction, which ensures a overall engagement and participation of all the employees in the safety culture. Apart from workplace safety, HEL is committed to maintaining convenient and congenial office place, canteen and communication system.

Social Wellness

Haldia Energy Limited fosters an environment that helps to develop a sense of connection, belongingness amongst its employees and their families and a well-developed support system that binds together the employees at HEL. HEL hosts sports tournament, Annual picnic, foundation day celebrations, milestone celebrations, ladies club activities, film festivals, etc. to ensures social wellness with employees and their families. HEL has also made residential complex (Quest Tower) for comfortable accommodation of its employees

Emotional Wellness

Relationship at work between peers & subordinate is something that makes a person emotionally stable. The cohesiveness score within different teams is one of the highest. Group activities like Family Get Together, New Year Celebration, Holi Milan, Birthday Celebration, and Annual Picnic are being organized to enhance camaraderie among the employees. Employees participate in different sports and games organized throughout the year.
